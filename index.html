Pushing The Comfort Zone

Our criteria for the best product rests on function, repairability, and, foremost, durability. Among the most direct ways we can limit ecological impacts is with goods that last for generations or can be recycled so the materials in them remain in use. Making the best product matters for saving the planet.

Build the best product

Spandex is a critical material in our products. Also known as elastane or Lycra, spandex is an elastic polymer (a type of plastic derived from petroleum) that makes garments and trims stretchy, which allows for them to be form fitting, and provide freedom of movement. Unfortunately, extracting and refining the petroleum used to make this material pollutes land, air and water and contributes to global warming.
